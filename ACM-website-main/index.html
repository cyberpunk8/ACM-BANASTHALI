About Us

Benefits of membership of ACM


  • 01 Learning

    Learning Center Learning Center with resources for lifelong learning, online books & videos.

  • 02 Library

    ACM Digital Library Subscription to the full ACM Digital Library for journals, conference proceedings, technical magazines, newsletters and books.

  • 03 Professional Development 

    Organizing workshops on emerging technologies.
    Hosting guest lectures and tech talks .
    Providing mentorship programs for students.

  • 04 Networking Opportunities 

    Connecting students with professionals, alumni, and ACM members worldwide.
    Collaborating with other student chapters and organizations.
    Organizing meetups, hackathons, and conferences.

  • 05 Competitions & Events 

    Conducting coding competitions (e.g., Hackathons, Codeathons, Capture The Flag).
    Encouraging participation in ACM.
    Hosting research paper presentations and poster sessions.

  • 06 Research & Learning Initiatives 

    Encouraging students to publish research papers in ACM journals and conferences.
    Promoting open-source contributions and collaborative projects.

  • 07 Career Support 

    Organizing resume-building and interview preparation sessions.
    Providing internship and job opportunities through industry partnerships.
    Running career guidance and alumni interaction programs.
